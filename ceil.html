Gypsum Ceilings

Gypsum false ceiling is a hydrated sulfate of calcium. This type of false ceiling are lightweight, sound insulated, fire resistance, soft and thermally insulated.
Gypsum false ceiling comes in the shape of square boards that are hung with the help of iron framework. The finishing work on these boards like paints, laminates, wallpapers and texture finish gives good look.These are largely used in spaces that require a centrally air-conditioned system.
Gypsum boards are easy to install and clean. These boards can provide a seamless look to your ceiling without too many joints
Since these are manufactured in factory machines, the consistency of the product’s finish and quality is maintained. It provides excellent thermal and acoustic properties while providing undulation free line.These ceilings are free from shrinkage cracks. It has low thermal conductivity and hence it helps in keeping your houses cool in summer and hot in winters which in turn save energy cost.
Rs.75-120/sq-ft

Plaster of Paris(POP) Ceiling

Plaster of Paris (POP) is the most common & majorly used among all false ceiling types! The gypsum, which gives out the water when heated, makes the POP. Post-heating, gypsum softens & is then crushed to form the powder.
POP hardens instantly when water is added to it. Plaster of Paris drop ceiling looks attractive & has a very long lifespan. They are extremely excellent insulators to heat & cold.
This kind of drop ceiling or false ceiling not only hide the hideous members of construction, ventilation ducts, & conduits but additionally give a smooth finish to your ceiling.POP is highly durable and can sustain for years without any wear or tear.
POP can be moulded in various designs and is flexible to apply on the corners.The most important feature of this false ceiling’s beauty is an indirect form of artificial light.They are best known for the aesthetic appeal and flexibility with recesses and different levels.
POP Ceilings are usually finished with application of paints of the client's colour choice. White paints are the most predominant choice when POP Ceilings are used.
The same systems are used for partitions and vertical elements but may not require suspension to an existing surface but anchorage where required.
Rs:120-125/sq-ft

Metal Ceilings

Metal ceilings are available in a wide range of variations. These include linear, tiled, open and closed cell systems all of which can be specified in a range of shapes and curves depending on the carrier system used.
Metal panels are stronger and less prone to cracks and breaks that traditional dropped ceiling materials while still offering easy access to the ceiling void. Metal ceilings are also moisture resistant which mitigates staining and crumbling from leaks.
The ceiling panels offer enhanced acoustics which can be substantially improved with the addition of special acoustic pads.But metal ceilings are available that help absorb or redirect sound so you don’t create an echo chamber.Ceilings with heavy inlays also attenuate sound from adjoining areas.The acoustic comfort in an office space, school or public building contributes to our well being as poor acoustic performance can affect health, communication, safety, productivity and learning.
These are available in a range of standard colours and can be coated in any RAL color.
It ensures the highest standard of production processes, material, machinery and finished product.All of our metal ceiling systems earnt a high reaction to fire

PVC Ceiling

Product description:-
PVC False Ceiling Panel are the ideal way to deliver tempered air from preheated hallways to farrowing or nursery rooms. The hollow profile acts as an air plenum delivering the air through precisely sized and positioned holes.
The air enters the room uniformly without creating drafts It is cleanable, non-absorbent surface is UV stable and will not discolour.
PVC ceiling panels solves the problem of dirty, unhygienic ceilings that are difficult, and expensive to clean by providing a bright, durable and hygienic surface, which is easily maintained·
PVC ceiling panels is light in weight, moisture resistant and reduces condensation and subsequent dripping from ceiling onto work surfaces
This extremely practical ceiling system is suitable for any area requiring a fixed, non-porous, non-fibrous and non-absorbent ceiling. PVC ceilings offer excellent insulation to keep you extra warm in the winter season.
Pvc ceilings are water proof and fire retardant. PVC ceiling are available in matt or gloss and are suitable for humid and dry environments.
The best advantages of pvc ceilings is that they last for years and they are maintenance free! Pvc Ceiling Boards Price difference compared to gypsum ceilings will be less heavy on your pocket.

Cement-Fibre Ceiling

False Ceiling Fibre Cement Board is the perfect choice for building board applications such as wall cladding, wall partitioning and decorative walling. The types of buildings that are suitable for False Ceiling Fibre Cement Board applications are residential, commercial and industrial buildings as well as schools and hospitals especially where speedy construction, cleanliness and hygiene is of high importance.
It is a non-asbestos fibre-cement product composed of Portland cement, cellulose fibre and refined sand. Using a special manufacturing process called autoclave. It acquires the strength, durability of cement and easy workability of wood as well as dimensional stability.
A non-combustible multi-purpose application fibre-cement flat board and comes with various thicknesses which are suitable for various applications; ceiling, wall, and floor. It can be used for both exterior and interior applications.
Those who live near the ocean often install it because it holds up well against the harsh sea salt air and can withstand extreme weather wind and rain for many years. More than 90 percent of fiber cement’s composition is not flammable, which is another reason it is so popular.
The cement composition also makes the material immune to insects and rot, and because there are no overlapping seams, mold and mildew cannot grow, making the home safer overall. Finally, fiber cement is versatile.
It isAvailable in a wide variety of colors and it can mimic wood shingles or planks and also can be painted to look even more unique
- Price:Rs 60-150/sq-ft
